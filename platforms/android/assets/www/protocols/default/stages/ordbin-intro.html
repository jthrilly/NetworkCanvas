Collecting ordinal & categorical data

Network Canvas has also improved upon the process of collecting ordinal and categorical data quickly and effectively.

The following interface allows respondents to quickly drag nodes into predefined bins, which we believe to be both efficient and low-burden relative to grid or matrix based approaches.

The visual nature of the interface also allows reflection on previous choices, including the ability to subsequently re-allocate a node to a different bin. This has the potential to increase data quality.

The number of bins, and the values that they assign as alter attributes, are all configurable parameters of this interface.

On the following screen, using a mouse or your fingers (if on a touchscreen device) drag the nodes into an appropriate bin.

Click the arrow on the right to continue.
