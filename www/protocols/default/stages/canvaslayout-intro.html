Drawing a sociogam

The next step represents one of the aspects of Network Canvas we are most excited about. Building on prior work in both Sociology, SNA, and HCI, we have attempted to provide a highly configurable digital canvas for participant-aided sociogram creation.

The "sociogram stage" can be configured to show any number of concentric circles, any number of prompts (or tasks), and multiple different types of activity, including edge linking, alter nomination, and node layout.

On the following screen, using a mouse or your fingers (if on a touch screen device) drag nodes to position them.

Click the arrow on the right to continue.
