Introduction

This demonstration shows some of the features of Network Canvas. The demonstration is based on the 2004 General Social Survey

We have made some slight changes to these questions. If you are interested, contact us to see the differences and a discussion of these.

Please note:

Data from this demonstration will be stored locally and will appear next time you open Network Canvas unless you explicitly reset session. Data is never uploaded to a third party. This is done by clicking the gear icoln in the top left, and selecting "reset session".
